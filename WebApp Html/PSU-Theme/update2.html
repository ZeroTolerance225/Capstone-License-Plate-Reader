Update 2: Security Enhancements and Bug Fixes

Published on March 15, 2024

We're excited to announce Update 2 for PennState Plates, focusing on enhancing security measures and addressing several key bugs identified by our community.

Security Enhancements

We've implemented additional layers of security to protect user data, including:

  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for all accounts
  • Encrypted data storage for sensitive information

Bug Fixes

Thanks to your feedback, we've addressed the following issues:

- Fixed a login issue affecting some users
- Resolved an error with license plate recognition in low-light conditions
- Corrected a dashboard display error on mobile devices

For a detailed view of the changes, visit our changelog.

How to Update

To apply Update 2, navigate to your account settings and click "Check for Updates." Follow the prompts to ensure your application is up-to-date.

As always, we're here to help. If you encounter any issues or have feedback, please reach out to our support team.
